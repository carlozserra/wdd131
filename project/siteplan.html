Project Site Plan

Site Name

Pedal Bike

This name was chosen because it represents a brand that focuses on trail bikes and cycling experiences. The site is dedicated to providing information about trail biking, bike maintenance, and bike purchase options.

Site Purpose

The website's main purpose is to serve as a hub for trail bike enthusiasts. It will offer information on the best biking trails, bike maintenance, and allow users to schedule maintenance services or buy bikes directly from the website.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the best trail bikes to purchase?
  • How can I schedule a bike maintenance service?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (Black): Used for headers and major text elements.
  • Secondary Color (Yellow): Used for highlights and accent elements like buttons and links.
  • Background Color (Light Gray): Used for the main background color to provide contrast with the text.

Typography

  • Arial (Sans-Serif): Used for body text to ensure readability across devices.
  • Georgia (Serif): Used for headings to give a classic, elegant look that contrasts with the modern sans-serif body text.
